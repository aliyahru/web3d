3D Modelling

Even before Web 3D, I enjoyed 3D modelling in Blender. I started off creating GFXs using Blender and then moved onto creating my own models. My preferred style of shading models is a more cartoony style rather than realistic. This style doesn't carry over well into Three js as it utilises Blender's emission shader, so the models emitting light doesn't look right when imported into Three JS.
